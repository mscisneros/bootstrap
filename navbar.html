Navbar

The navbar includes support for branding, navigation, search, utilities and more.

  • Created and set the background container to 1440px wide and 78px in height, renamed the layer to background-color, and then applied an existing Color Layer Styles to it.
  • I inserted a Logo Symbol and then scaled it to 48x48px dimensions, aligned 56px from the left edge, 16px from the top, and bottom.
  • For the “Navigation Items”, I added one of the existing Text Styles, aligned it 16pt from the right of the Logo and then fixed its size both horizontally, and vertically.
  • I simply dropped in a handful of Icon Symbols, and spaced them 16pt apart from one another with the far right icon sitting 56pt from the right edge of the navigation bar.

Important: With an abundance of “Overrides” at your disposal you can easily go ahead and tweak this Component based on your project's requirements.
